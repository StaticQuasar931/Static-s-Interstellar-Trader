Welcome to Interstellar Trader! 🚀

📈 Objective: Build an intergalactic trading empire by sending your ships to trade goods between planets.

🛠️ Upgrades: Purchase upgrades to enhance your technology, increase profits, automate your trading operations, and more.

🔄 Rebirth: Reset your progress to gain Rebirth Points (RP) which can be spent on powerful upgrades.

🔍 Buttons and Upgrades Explained:

  • Send All Ships to Trade (⚙️): Initiates trade missions for all available ships simultaneously.
  • Buy a New Ship (⚖️): Purchase additional ships to expand your trading fleet.
  • Buy Upgrade (✚): Enhance your technology level, increasing earnings per trade.
  • Upgrade All (🔼): Upgrade all ships based on the selected buy amount, prioritizing cheaper upgrades first.
  • Upgrade Menu (🔧): Access various upgrade options including Automation, Multiplier, Employee Hiring, Big Upgrades, and Ship Limit enhancements.

Upgrade Options:

  • Buy Automation (🤖): Unlock automation for your ships, allowing them to trade automatically without manual intervention.
  • Buy Multiplier (✖️): Increase your technology level multiplier, significantly boosting earnings per trade.
  • Hire Employee (👨‍🚀): Hire specific types of employees to assist with trading, each type offering unique benefits.
  • Big Upgrade for Ship (🚀): Apply a Big Upgrade to a specific ship, doubling its trader level and enhancing its trading speed.
  • Upgrade Ship Limit (📈): Increase the maximum number of ships you can own, allowing for fleet expansion.

Employee Types:

  • Storage Employee (📦): Increases your ship storage capacity, allowing you to own more ships.
  • Efficiency Employee (⚙️): Enhances trade efficiency, reducing trade times and increasing earnings.
  • Security Employee (🛡️): Protects your ships from random events that may cause credit loss.
