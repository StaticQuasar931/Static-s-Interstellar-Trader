🔼 Upgrade Menu

Select upgrades to enhance your trading capabilities:

  • Automate Trades (🤖): Enable automatic trading for your ships.
  • Increase Multiplier (✖️): Enhance your trading multiplier for increased earnings.
  • Big Upgrade for Ship (🚀): Apply a Big Upgrade to a specific ship, doubling its trader level and enhancing its trading speed.
  • Upgrade Ship Limit (📈): Increase the maximum number of ships you can own, allowing for fleet expansion.

Upgrade Costs:

  • 🤖 Automation: Cost varies based on current automation levels.
  • ✖️ Multiplier: Cost increases with each purchase.
  • 🚀 Big Upgrade: Cost scales with technology level.
  • 📈 Ship Limit: Cost scales with current ship limit.
