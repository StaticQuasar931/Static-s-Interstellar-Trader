Upgrade Menu

Available Upgrades:

  • Automation (🤖): Automate trading for your ships, allowing them to trade automatically without manual intervention.
  • Multiplier (✖️): Increase your credit multiplier, boosting earnings from all trade missions.
  • Big Upgrade for Ship (🚀): Apply a Big Upgrade to a specific ship, doubling its trader level and enhancing its trading speed.
  • Upgrade Ship Limit (📈): Increase the maximum number of ships you can own, allowing for fleet expansion.

Employee Types:

  • Storage Employee (📦): Increases your ship storage capacity, allowing you to own more ships.
  • Efficiency Employee (⚙️): Enhances trade efficiency, reducing trade times and increasing earnings.
  • Security Employee (🛡️): Protects your ships from random events that may cause credit loss.
